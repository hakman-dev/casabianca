Our Pool

Enjoy our refreshing pool

Our spacious pool is the perfect place to relax and enjoy the Spanish sun. The pool is shared with the owners and is accessible from 8:00 AM to 8:00 PM.

  • 2 sunbeds with cushions included
  • Black garden set available at the pool
  • Children without a swimming certificate must wear armbands or life jacket
  • Parents/guardians must always be present
  • Maximum depth approximately 1.70 meters (diving not allowed)
  • Showering before swimming is mandatory
